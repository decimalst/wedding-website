General Information/FAQ

How do I get there?

To get to St. John, guests should fly into St. Thomas (Airport code STT). Upon arrival, guests will take a taxi to the Red Hook Ferry Terminal. (Approximate cost for the taxi is $15-$20). The ferry leaves every hour on the hour to St. John from 6am to midnight. Ferry fare is $8.15 per person and $4 per bag.

How long should I stay?

Kelly and Byron will be staying in St. John from Thursday, March 18 - Tuesday, March 23rd. We will have optional activities planned for everyone, but you are not expected to be anywhere (other than the wedding of course!). You are welcome to stay for as long or as little as you'd like. We just appreciate you making the trip down! If you can only join us for a limited time, we suggest a Saturday 3/20 - Monday 3/22 trip, as the wedding will be held on Sunday evening.

Is there a hotel block? Are all guests staying in the same resort??

There is not a formal hotel block, and guests will not all be staying in the same place. Kelly and Byron will be staying in a private beach villa. Guests can opt to either rent their own villa, or guests can stay at a hotel on the island. Please see the "Accommodations" section of the website. Our wedding planner & coordinator is more than happy to help you with your accommodation planning if needed!

What if I want to stay on St. Thomas?

You are welcome to do that! We recommend St. John since we will be there and we will be planning activities for everyone there, but if you would prefer St. Thomas, you can take the ferry over to St. John when needed. St. Thomas has many hotel options, typically at a lower price point than St. John.

Do I need to rent a car?

You can make it work without a car! there are taxis on the island of St. John, and we are working to provide travel from your hotels to and from our wedding venue. However, since St. John has many different beaches and landmarks to visit across the island and guests will not be together in one hotel, we suggest a rental car may make your stay easier if you are staying for more than a couple days.

What if I have more questions?

Feel free to contact our wedding planner, Michelle Cawthron, with any questions you may have. Her email is Michelle@paradiseplanningUSVI.com. And of course feel free to reach out to Kelly or Byron directly!
